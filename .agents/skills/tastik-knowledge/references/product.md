# Product Vision

## Tech Stack
- Convex (backend and data)
- Better Auth (authentication)
- Stripe (subscriptions and billing)

## Positioning
- Tastik fills the gap for casual, ongoing tasks that do not need due dates or complex project management.

## Target User
- People managing everyday, ongoing tasks like groceries, side projects, bill tracking, and monthly expenses.

## Business Model
- Hard paywall: pay or don't use it.
- Pricing: $1.99/month or $19.99/year.
- Goal is self-sustaining and to cover hosting costs.
